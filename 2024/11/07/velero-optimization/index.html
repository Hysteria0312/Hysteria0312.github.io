Background

When the bottleneck in backup processing speed lies in read and write operations between memory and disk, restore generates a large volume of network requests. For companies with large clusters, restore performance becomes a critical issue.

Scenario

Because of high availability of the control plane, architects decided to store key information about tenants in etcd. However, the massive volume of data has made Velero incapable of handling the restore process effectively.
